The Mission To Unite Earth Under The Stars

UESA is the first global, open-source space agency where people from all countries can contribute to humanity’s exploration of the cosmos. Humanity has explored the darkness of space with the light of science and technology. Long ago, people looked at the night sky, wondering what’s out there. Now we know, and we should adopt a mindset focused on evolving into the most advanced intelligence in the universe. That’s our mission — humanity’s mission.

This is where we all live: Earth. And if we want to conquer space, we first have to save our home — our planet — which provides everything we use to advance technology. All those resources, sites, and places where we learn and live exist on Earth. So Earth should be our first priority as well. This mentality is futuristic, practical, and the only realistic way humanity can avoid dying out like the dinosaurs.

SORA
