The Mission To Unite Earth Under The Stars

UESA is the first global, open-source space agency — where people from all countries can contribute to humanity’s exploration of the cosmos. Humanity has explored the darkness of space with the light of science and technology. Ages ago, people looked at the night sky, wondering what's up there. Now we know, and we should have a mindset of evolving into the most advanced intelligence in the Universe. That's our mission - humanity's mission.

This is where all of us are: Earth. And if we want to conquer space, we first have to save our own home, our planet, which gives us everything we use to advance in technology. All those resources, sites, places where we learn, places we live, all exist on Earth. So our first priority should be Earth too! This mentality is futuristic, practical, and actually the only way humanity would realistically do it if we were serious about not dying out like the dinosaurs.
